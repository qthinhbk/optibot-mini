To add a YouTube video to your OptiSigns screens, follow these steps:

* Log in to the [OptiSigns portal](http://app.optisigns.com/).
* Go to **Files/Assets**, click **App**, and select **YouTube**.
* Enter a name for the asset and paste the direct URL to your YouTube video in the **URL** field.
* Check the **Caption** box if you wish to display subtitles (note: these must be created by the video author).
* Click **Save** and assign the asset to your screen, playlist, or schedule.

For more details, please see these articles:
- [How to use YouTube with OptiSigns](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360051014713)
- [Show YouTube video's subtitle/closed caption](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360051014773)
