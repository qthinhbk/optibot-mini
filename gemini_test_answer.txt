To add a YouTube video to your OptiSigns screen, follow these steps:

*   Log in to your portal at [app.optisigns.com](http://app.optisigns.com/).
*   Navigate to **Files/Assets**, click **App**, and select **YouTube**.
*   Enter a name for the asset and paste the **URL** of the YouTube video.
*   Check the **Caption** box if you wish to display subtitles (provided they were created by the video author).
*   Click **Save** and assign the asset to your screen, playlist, or schedule.
